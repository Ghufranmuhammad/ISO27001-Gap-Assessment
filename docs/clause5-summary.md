ISO 27001 Clause 5 Summary

Clause 5.1 – Leadership and Commitment

Top management must demonstrate leadership and commitment by supporting the Information Security Management System (ISMS). They provide resources, establish security objectives, promote continual improvement, and ensure information security is integrated into business processes.

Example: The CEO of DesertTech Solutions approves the security budget, supports employee awareness training, and participates in management review meetings.

---

Clause 5.2 – Information Security Policy

The organization must establish an Information Security Policy that provides direction for information security and supports business objectives. The policy must be communicated to employees and reviewed regularly.

Example: DesertTech Solutions publishes an Information Security Policy that all employees must read and follow.

---

Clause 5.3 – Organizational Roles, Responsibilities, and Authorities

Management must clearly define and communicate security roles and responsibilities throughout the organization.

Example: The CEO approves security strategy, the IT Manager implements technical controls, the GRC Analyst manages compliance activities, and employees follow security policies.
