# ISO 27001 Clause 7 Summary

## 7.1 Resources

DesertTech Solutions provides qualified staff, security tools, software, budget, and time to support the ISMS.

---

## 7.2 Competence

Employees receive appropriate education, certifications, and security training before performing security-related responsibilities.

---

## 7.3 Awareness

Employees receive regular awareness training covering phishing, password security, data classification, and company policies.

---

## 7.4 Communication

The company communicates important security information through email, meetings, awareness campaigns, and management announcements.

---

## 7.5 Documented Information

All ISMS documents are version-controlled, approved, securely stored, reviewed regularly, and retained according to company policy.
