ISMS Scope

Organization

DesertTech Solutions LLC

Business Scope

The ISMS covers the delivery of IT consulting, cloud services, managed IT support, cybersecurity services, and software development.

Physical Location

Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates

Departments Included

- Human Resources
- Finance
- Information Technology
- Sales
- Operations

Information Assets Included

- Customer information
- Employee records
- Financial records
- Email systems
- Cloud infrastructure
- Company laptops
- Internal business applications

Exclusions

No branch offices outside Abu Dhabi are included in the current ISMS scope.

Scope Statement

The Information Security Management System (ISMS) applies to DesertTech Solutions LLC's operations located in Abu Dhabi. It covers all employees, business processes, information assets, and supporting technologies used to deliver IT and cybersecurity services. The ISMS is designed to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of company and customer information while supporting business objectives and regulatory compliance.
