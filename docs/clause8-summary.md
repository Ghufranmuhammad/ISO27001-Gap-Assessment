# ISO 27001:2022 Clause 8 Summary

## Clause 8 – Operation

Clause 8 ensures that the Information Security Management System (ISMS) is operated effectively. It focuses on implementing planned activities, performing risk assessments, treating identified risks, and monitoring controls.

---

## 8.1 Operational Planning and Control

### Summary

The organization must ensure that planned information security activities are implemented and controlled. Security controls should operate consistently, responsibilities should be assigned, and evidence should be maintained.

### DesertTech Solutions Example

DesertTech Solutions requires Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Microsoft 365. The IT Manager enables MFA, verifies it is active for all users, stores implementation evidence, and reviews compliance monthly.

### Connection to Risk Register

Operational planning ensures that controls selected during the risk assessment are actually implemented and monitored.

---

## 8.2 Information Security Risk Assessment

### Summary

The organization identifies information security risks by evaluating assets, threats, vulnerabilities, likelihood, and business impact.

### DesertTech Solutions Example

The customer database is vulnerable to ransomware due to delayed patching. The likelihood is rated High and the impact is Critical, resulting in a High Risk rating.

### Connection to Risk Register

Each identified risk is recorded in the Risk Register together with its owner, likelihood, impact, and overall risk rating.

---

## 8.3 Information Security Risk Treatment

### Summary

Organizations decide how each identified risk will be managed by reducing, avoiding, transferring, or accepting the risk.

### DesertTech Solutions Example

To reduce phishing attacks, DesertTech implements Multi-Factor Authentication, advanced email filtering, and mandatory employee awareness training.

### Connection to Risk Register

Risk treatment updates the Risk Register by documenting selected controls, responsible owners, implementation status, and residual risk after treatment.

---

## Key Learning

Clause 8 transforms information security planning into operational activities. It ensures that risks are managed effectively and that implemented controls are continuously monitored and improved.
