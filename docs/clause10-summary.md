# ISO 27001:2022 Clause 10 Summary

## Clause 10 – Improvement

Clause 10 ensures that the Information Security Management System (ISMS) continually improves by addressing nonconformities, identifying root causes, implementing corrective actions, and monitoring effectiveness.

---

## 10.1 Continual Improvement

### Summary

Organizations should continuously improve the effectiveness, suitability, and adequacy of the ISMS using audit findings, incidents, risk assessments, management reviews, and performance data.

### DesertTech Solutions Example

DesertTech improved its phishing awareness program after employees repeatedly failed phishing simulations. Monthly training and simulations reduced the failure rate significantly.

---

## 10.2 Nonconformity and Corrective Action

### Summary

Organizations must identify nonconformities, determine their root causes, implement corrective actions, verify effectiveness, and retain documented evidence.

### DesertTech Solutions Example

The quarterly Risk Register review was missed. Investigation found there was no documented review schedule. A recurring quarterly review calendar and assigned owner were introduced.

---

## Correction vs Corrective Action

Correction fixes the immediate issue.

Corrective action eliminates the root cause to prevent recurrence.
