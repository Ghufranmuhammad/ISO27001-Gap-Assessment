# ISO 27001 Clause 6 Summary

## Clause 6.1 – Actions to Address Risks and Opportunities

Organizations must identify information security risks and opportunities, assess their impact and likelihood, and decide how to treat them. Risk treatment helps reduce risks to an acceptable level.

**Risk Assessment:** Risk assessment is the process of identifying risks, evaluating their likelihood and impact, and prioritizing them for treatment.

**Risk Treatment Options:**
- Reduce the risk by implementing controls.
- Avoid the risk by stopping the activity.
- Transfer the risk to another party (e.g., insurance).
- Accept the risk if it is within the organization's risk appetite.

---

## Clause 6.2 – Information Security Objectives

Organizations must establish measurable information security objectives that align with business goals and support continual improvement.

---

## Clause 6.3 – Planning of Changes

Changes to the ISMS should be planned carefully to ensure they do not introduce unnecessary risks.

---

## Examples (DesertTech Solutions LLC)

### Example 1
Risk: Employees fall victim to phishing emails.

Treatment: Conduct phishing awareness training and enable email filtering.

---

### Example 2
Risk: Company laptops are stolen.

Treatment: Enable full-disk encryption and remote wipe capability.

---

### Example 3
Risk: Cloud storage is misconfigured.

Treatment: Perform regular cloud security reviews and restrict administrator access.
