# Enterprise Security Awareness & Training Plan

## 1. Roles and Responsibilities
*   **CISO (Program Executive):** Holds structural accountability, approves budget allocation, and presents quarterly progress metrics to the Board.
*   **Security Training Coordinator:** Designs curricula, manages the Learning Management System (LMS), and tracks completion logs.
*   **Department Heads / Line Managers:** Enforce mandatory completion deadlines across direct reports.
*   **All Personnel:** Complete assigned training blocks and report anomalous activity to the SOC.

## 2. Audience Segmentation & Training Methods
*   **All Employees / Contractors:** Short, interactive 10-minute web modules focusing on foundational security habits.
*   **High-Risk Personnel (Finance & HR):** Target-driven workshops tackling targeted spear-phishing and payment fraud schemes.
*   **Technical / Engineering Personnel:** Secure coding certifications (OWASP Top 10) integrated directly into technical career paths.

## 3. Annual Training Calendar
*   **Q1 (January - March):** Core Information Security Fundamentals & Password Management.
*   **Q2 (April - June):** Social Engineering, Phishing Vectors, & Vishing.
*   **Q3 (July - September):** Clean Desk Policy, Removable Media, & Remote Work Security.
*   **Q4 (October - December):** Incident Reporting Protocols & Annual Assessment Exam.

## 4. Topics Covered
*   Phishing detection mechanisms.
*   Safe browsing and public Wi-Fi safety patterns.
*   Data categorization rules (Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted).
*   物理的セキュリティ (Tailgating and clean desk constraints).

## 5. Success Metrics & Targets
*   **LMS Completion Rate:** Greater than 95% total organizational completion within 30 days of campaign deployment.
*   **Phishing Simulation Click Rate:** Reduce corporate click rate baseline below 3% within 12 months.
*   **Reporting Velocity:** Lift user reporting rates from discovery to ticket submittal under 15 minutes.
