# Document Control Procedure

## Purpose

Ensure all ISMS documents are properly managed throughout their lifecycle.

---

## Version Control

Every document receives a version number whenever updated.

---

## Document Review

Documents are reviewed annually or whenever major changes occur.

---

## Approval Process

Documents must be approved by management before publication.

---

## Storage

Documents are stored securely in the organization's document repository with restricted access.

---

## Retention

Documents are retained according to legal, regulatory, and business requirements.

---

## Disposal

Obsolete documents are securely deleted or destroyed after the retention period expires.
