# Security Awareness Plan

## Objectives

Increase employee awareness of information security threats and reduce human-related security risks.

---

## Target Audience

- Employees
- Managers
- IT Staff
- Contractors

---

## Training Topics

- Phishing Awareness
- Password Security
- Data Classification
- Social Engineering
- Safe Internet Usage
- Remote Working Security

---

## Delivery Method

- Online Training
- Classroom Sessions
- Email Campaigns
- Posters
- Phishing Simulations

---

## Frequency

Quarterly training with monthly reminders.

---

## Success Metrics

- 100% training completion
- Reduced phishing click rate
- Improved security awareness quiz scores
