# ISO 27001 Gap Assessment Scope

## Organization
DesertTech Solutions LLC

## In-Scope Systems
* Employee workstations
* Cloud storage systems
* Internal business applications
* Email and communication systems

## In-Scope Departments
* IT Department
* HR Department
* Finance Department
* Operations Team

## Out of Scope
* Third-party vendor systems
* Personal employee devices (BYOD not assessed in detail)
* Physical security of external data centers

## Reason for Scope Limitation
The assessment focuses on core business systems that directly handle organizational data and are critical for ISO 27001 compliance evaluation.
