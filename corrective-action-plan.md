# Corrective Action Plan (CAPA)

## Finding 1

**Finding:** Employees failed to complete mandatory awareness training.

**Root Cause:** No automated reminder process.

**Immediate Correction:** Employees were enrolled in the next available training session.

**Long-Term Corrective Action:** Implement automated reminders and monthly completion reports for managers.

**Verification Method:** Review training completion reports and audit attendance records.

**Target Completion Date:** 15 August 2026

---

## Finding 2

**Finding:** Quarterly Risk Register review was missed.

**Root Cause:** No documented review schedule.

**Immediate Correction:** Conduct overdue review immediately.

**Long-Term Corrective Action:** Create a documented quarterly review schedule with calendar reminders and assigned ownership.

**Verification Method:** Verify completed review meeting minutes every quarter.

**Target Completion Date:** 31 July 2026
